Teaching

I teach in the area of public policy, political participation and political organisations in Germany and from a European perspective. I also teach courses on research methods, research strategies and academic writing. In addition to academic courses I have been leading seminars and workshops in adult education for several years. If you are interested in practical courses in any of these areas, please contact me.
