Teaching

I teach in the area of political participation, parties and political organisations in Germany and from a European perspective. Additionally, I am covering courses on social science methodology, research strategies and academic writing. Besides academic courses I have been leading seminars and workshops in adult education for several years. If you are interested in very practically oriented courses in any of the areas get in touch with me.
